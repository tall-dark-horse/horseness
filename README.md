# horseness.com

The website for [horseness.com](https://horseness.com) — a philosophy of self, inspired by the concept of *horseness* as explored in *Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance* by Robert M. Pirsig, and extended by [Sudheesh Nair](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/horseness-sudheesh-nair).

> *"Every horse is simply being a horse — and in doing so, it becomes the living definition of horseness for everyone who encounters it. You are someone's first encounter with what you love."*

---

## The idea

Horseness is the philosophical essence of a horse — what makes a horse a horse, independent of any particular horse. The human extension of this idea: your passions, quirks, and enthusiasms may be the only version of a concept that the people around you ever encounter. You are their reference point. That is reason enough to share what you love.
